## Features of the Basic Bible App

This list includes all current functionalities and those in active development for the upcoming release.

---

### **Core Reading & Navigation**
* **Universal Format Support:** Native parsing and rendering for **USFX**, **OSIS**, and **Zefania** XML files.
* **Advanced Reference Picker:** Navigate via a full-screen picker featuring:
    * Instant search
    * Canonical or alphabetical ordering
    * Recent-reference history
    * Optional verse-level selection
* **Three Reading Modes:**
    * **Verse List:** Standard verse-by-verse view.
    * **Document Mode:** Optimized for narrative flow.
    * **Continuous Scrolling:** A whole-Bible lazy reader that keeps your place.
* **Dynamic Theming:** High-contrast and comfort-focused themes including:
    * **System, Light, and Dark**
    * **Soft Dark, Pure Black (OLED), and Pure White**
    * **Blue and Red** accents

---

### **High-Fidelity Study Tools**
* **Rich Text Rendering:** Deep support for technical Bible formatting, including:
    * **Words of Jesus** (Red Letter support)
    * **Translator Additions** (Italicized with brackets)
    * **Poetry & Prose** (Indentation and structure preservation)
    * **Section Headings** (Level-aware styles like `<ms>`, `<s>`, `<s2>`)
* **Interactive Annotations:** * **Footnotes & Cross-References:** Structured, tappable markers that open in dedicated sheets.
    * **Strong's Integration:** Metadata for original language study and word morphology.
* **Personal Study Engine:**
    * Save **standalone highlights** or **linked personal notes**.
    * Preserve the specific translation used when adding verses to a note.
    * Dedicated **Notes & Highlights** screen for management and editing.

---

### **Advanced Functionality (Coming Soon)**
* **Cloud Sync:** Seamlessly synchronize your library and notes across mobile, desktop, and web.
* **Audio & TTS:** Integrated audio playback and Text-to-Speech support for on-the-go listening.
* **Smart Bible Study:** * **Daily Verse & Habit Tracking:** Built-in features to encourage consistent reading.
    * **Prayer Lists:** Manage and track your personal prayer requests within the app.
* **Expanded Web Support:** Browser-based import/export for local SQLite and XML Bible databases.
* **Accessibility Polish:** Full screen-reader and accessibility optimization for all users.
* **Native Share & Export:** Export your study notes and highlighted verses to external apps or social media.

---

### **Technical Infrastructure**
* **Local-First Persistence:** Parsed content is stored locally on your device for instant offline access.
* **Multi-Platform Ready:** Built with Flutter for a consistent experience on **Android, iOS, Linux, Windows, macOS, and Web**.
* **Open Library Management:** Switch between bundled, cached, and locally imported translations with ease.
